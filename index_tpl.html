Why it's being sold

We've had a lot of fun with the van over the last two years: We repaired many small problems, redid all the upholstery, and installed additional instruments. The van took us successfully to camp spots around California. It wasn't always the fastest vehicle on the road but it did always take us reliably.

We do however have only one garage parking spot, just started new jobs, and don't have quite the time to have as much fun with the van as we'd like to. For that reason, we're willing to let it go to a new home.
